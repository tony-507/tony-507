Triangle to rectangle

Here we try to convert a given triangle to a rectangle with same base and same area. The construction is simple: $$ \frac{1}{2}ab = a\left(\frac{b}{2}\right). $$

To be specific, we do the following.

  1. Find the altitude to the base. Denote the mid-point of the altitude by M.
  2. Draw a line parallel to the base through M.
  3. Draw two lines perpendicular to the base though end-points of the base.
