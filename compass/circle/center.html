Center of circle

Given a circle, we want to determine its center. We use the fact that perpendicular bisector of chord passes through the center.

  1. Fix three points A,B,C on the circle and connect AB and BC.
  2. Construct perpendicular bisectors of AB and BC. Label their intersection as O.
  3. O is the desired center of the circle.
