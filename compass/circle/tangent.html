Tangent

Given a circle and a point \(A\) on the circle, we want to find the tangent to the circle through \(A\). This can be done easily by the fact that tangent perpendicular radius.

  1. Locate the center \(O\) of the circle.
  2. Connect \(OA\).
  3. Construct perpendicular of \(OA\) at \(A\). It is the desired tangent.

We can also contruct a tangent by angle in alternate segment.

  1. Pick \(B,C\) on the circle. Connect \(AB, BC, AC\).
  2. Copy the angle \(\angle ABC\) to \(AC\). The other side of the angle is the tangent.
