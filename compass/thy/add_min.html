Addition and subtraction

Let's discuss the addition and subtraction of constructible numbers. These two are rather trivial. For addition:

  1. Copy the segment of length \(x\)
  2. Extend the segment of lemgth \(y\) by \(x\).

For subtraction:

  1. Copy the segment of length \(x\).
  2. Copy the remaining part of segment of length \(y\) after removing segment of length \(x\)
