In this website, I would like to explore straightedge and compass construction. I find this topic interesting and easy to learn. We only need high school geometry and basic abstract algebra to fully understand the context.

The web is structured as follows:

  1. Preliminaries: "Basics" and "Theories" serve as foundation of the topic. We discuss common constructions there. Understanding of the materials there helps to grasp the idea behind.
  2. Further reading: "Triangles" and "Circles" contain constructions related to triangles and circles. Theories behind constructions there are harder and usually employ tools in "Basics" section.
  3. Challenge: "Problems" contain some applications of materials in first two sections. You are required to do some tasks via tools in other parts.

Hope you find this interesting!
